# MAHJ — Legal Guardrails

This document defines the **non-negotiable rules** for what MAHJ may and may not include. Every contributor (human or AI) must follow these. If in doubt, ask before shipping.

> **This is not legal advice.** Before public launch or accepting payment, the project owner should consult a games/IP attorney for a formal review.

---

## 1. The NMJL card is off-limits

The National Mah Jongg League's official card is a copyrighted creative compilation that NMJL actively enforces. MAHJ **must not**:

- Reproduce the current or any past year's NMJL card hands
- Reproduce NMJL's specific layout, color coding, section ordering, or visual design
- Reproduce hand-naming conventions or notation taken directly from the card
- Imply MAHJ is a substitute for purchasing the official card

MAHJ **may**:

- Mention the NMJL card factually as required equipment
- Link users to [nationalmahjonggleague.org](https://www.nationalmahjonggleague.org) to purchase the official card
- Teach the *concept* of how American Mahjong cards generally work (categories, notation conventions used across the variant) using **original example hands**

## 2. Trademarks

MAHJ **must not** use as branding, app name, app store metadata, marketing copy, or in any user-facing surface:

- "NMJL" or "National Mah Jongg League"
- "I Love Mahj," "Real Mah Jongg," or any other existing app name
- Any other registered trademark in the mahjong space

MAHJ **may** reference NMJL by name *factually*, in disclaimer or instructional context only (e.g., "Buy the official card from the National Mah Jongg League").

## 3. Example hands

All hand examples in MAHJ:

- Must be **invented for teaching purposes**
- Must be labeled clearly as **"Practice Hand"** or **"Example Hand"**
- Must not match the structure of any specific hand from a current or past NMJL card
- Should use generic categories (e.g., "a consecutive run hand," "a pairs-only hand") rather than NMJL-specific naming

## 4. Tile artwork

All tile graphics in MAHJ:

- Must be **original work** (commissioned or created in-house), **OR**
- Must be sourced from a **clearly licensed** asset pack (CC0, CC-BY with attribution, or paid license)
- Must never be ripped from another mahjong app, website, or printed card

## 5. Required disclaimer

The following disclaimer (or substantively equivalent text) must appear:

- In the app footer on **every page**
- In the app store listing description
- In the project README
- In the Terms of Service / About page

> **MAHJ is an independent educational tool. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the National Mah Jongg League (NMJL®). NMJL® and the official National Mah Jongg League card are property of their respective owners. To play official American Mahjong, please purchase the current year's card from nationalmahjonggleague.org.**

## 6. What's safe (and what we build on)

The following are **not protected** and form the foundation of MAHJ:

- **Rules of the game** — rules of a game are not copyrightable
- **Generic tile concepts** — bams, cracks, dots, winds, dragons, flowers, jokers
- **Generic hand patterns** — pungs, kongs, quints, pairs, sequences as categories
- **The Charleston** as a procedure
- **Setup, dealing, breaking the wall** as procedures
- **Etiquette and table culture** descriptions
- **Original strategy, tips, and teaching content**
- **Universal card-reading notation conventions** (D for Dragon, NSEW for winds, F for Flower, etc.) that are common across all American Mahjong instruction
