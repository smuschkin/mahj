## Post 3 — QUICK QUIZ (dark green template)

**Label:** QUICK QUIZ
**Headline:** What's the Charleston?
**Subtitle:** Wrong answers welcome. Real answer in comments.
**Footer:** welcome2mahj.com

**Caption:**
No, it's not a dance (well, not in this context).

The Charleston is a tile-trading ritual that happens BEFORE the game even starts. You pass tiles you don't want and hopefully get ones you need. Right, Across, Left — then back again.

It's unique to American Mahjong and it's honestly one of the most fun parts.
