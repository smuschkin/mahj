# MAHJ TikTok Video Recording Guide
## Screen recordings + scripts for maximum reach

### HOW TO SCREEN RECORD ON IPHONE
1. Settings → Control Center → add "Screen Recording"
2. Swipe down from top-right → tap the record button
3. 3-second countdown, then it records everything on screen
4. Tap the red bar at top to stop
5. Video saves to Photos

### GENERAL TIPS
- Record in portrait (hold phone vertical)
- Turn on Do Not Disturb (no notifications during recording)
- Close other apps
- Make sure the app looks clean (full battery, no messy status bar)
- Record MORE than you need — you'll trim in editing
- Add text overlays and audio in CapCut or TikTok's editor
