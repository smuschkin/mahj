MAHJ — Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 10, 2026

Overview

MAHJ ("the App") is an educational tool for learning American Mahjong. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No account required. The App does not require sign-up, login, or any personal information.
  • No analytics or tracking. The App does not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking pixels.
  • No network requests. The App runs entirely on your device. It does not communicate with any server after download.

Local Storage

The App stores your learning progress (which modules you've completed, quiz scores) locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time by clearing your browser data or using the "Reset Progress" button in the App

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. It contains no user-generated content, social features, or external links beyond the NMJL official website.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services that collect data. No ads, no analytics, no social login.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices, we will update this page. The effective date at the top will reflect the latest revision.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at info@welcome2mahj.com.

MAHJ is an independent educational tool. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the National Mah Jongg League (NMJL).
